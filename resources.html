STEM Resources

The best websites, organisations, and tools to help you discover and secure your future in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Maths.

STEM Cymru

Runs the Engineering Education Scheme Wales (EESW) and offers fantastic hands-on STEM projects for students across Wales.

Visit Website

Careers Wales

The official Welsh Government careers portal. Excellent for finding local apprenticeships and understanding different STEM job profiles.

Visit Website

Gradcracker

The UK's top careers website for STEM students. While aimed mostly at university students, it's brilliant for researching which companies hire STEM graduates.

Visit Website

STEM SMART Cambridge

A free initiative from the University of Cambridge to support state school students in their STEM A-levels. Highly recommended.

Visit Website

Sutton Trust

Offers UK summer schools, US university programmes, and apprenticeships support for high-achieving students from less advantaged backgrounds.

Visit Website

Nuffield Research Placements

Provides engaging, real-world research or development placement opportunities in STEM fields for Year 12 students.

Visit Website
