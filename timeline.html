Your STEM Action Plan

Don't miss a deadline. Follow this step-by-step roadmap for Year 11 & Year 12 to secure the best apprenticeships and bursaries.

Y11

Year 11 / GCSE Level

Laying the foundation and exploring your options.

Autumn Term (Sep - Dec)

Research & Discovery

  • Start attending virtual open days for companies like GE Aerospace, Aston Martin, or local engineering hubs.
  • Look into post-16 options: Sixth Form vs. College vs. Level 3 Apprenticeships.
Spring Term (Jan - Apr)

Apply for Summer Insights

  • Many corporate "Insight Weeks" for 15-16 year olds close applications in February/March.
  • Draft your first basic CV. You don't need work experience—focus on school projects and skills.
Y12

Year 12 / First Year A-Level

The critical year for Degree Apprenticeships & Bursaries.

Autumn Term (Sep - Dec)

Early Applications Open

  • Top-tier Degree Apprenticeships (like BBC, Airbus, Network Rail) open now.
  • Register for the Nuffield Research Placements (crucial for Welsh students).
  • Start prepping for Psychometric Tests (companies use these to filter candidates).
Winter (Jan - Feb)

The Deadline Crunch

This is when 80% of major corporate schemes close. Do not leave applications to the last minute!

Check Live Deadlines
Summer Term (May - Jul)

Interviews & Assessment Centres

  • Companies will invite successful applicants to Assessment Centres.
  • Secure your Year 12 summer work experience placement.
