The Master STEM Roadmap

Y11

Year 11 / GCSE Level

Laying the foundation and exploring your options.

Autumn Term (Sep - Dec)

Research & Discovery

  • Start attending virtual open days for major engineering hubs and universities.
  • Look into post-16 options: Sixth Form vs. College vs. Level 3 Apprenticeships.
Spring Term (Jan - Apr)

Apply for Summer Insights

  • Corporate "Insight Weeks" for 15-16 year olds often close applications in February/March.
  • Draft your first basic CV. Focus on school projects, UKMT scores, and personal skills.
Y12

Year 12 / First Year A-Level

The critical year for Degree Apprenticeships, Exams, & Bursaries.

Autumn Term (Sep - Dec)

Early Applications & Olympiads

  • Top-tier Degree Apprenticeships (like BBC, Airbus) open now.
  • Keep an eye on registration dates for Senior Maths Challenges and Olympiads.
  • Register for Nuffield Research Placements (crucial for Welsh students).
Winter (Jan - Feb)

The Deadline Crunch

This is when 80% of major corporate schemes close. Do not leave applications to the last minute!

Save Live Deadlines to your Dashboard
Summer Term (May - Jul)

University Admissions Tests

  • Book your spot for specific university admissions tests (like ESAT or MAT) if required.
  • Secure your Year 12 summer work experience placement.
