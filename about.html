About The Founder

Austin Dallimore • Platform Developer

Welcome to the Rural Wales STEM Hub! This platform was built out of a direct need to simplify and accelerate how high-achieving student cohorts discover highly competitive, debt-free engineering, software, and industrial pathways.

As a developer based in Wales, I noticed that fantastic regional programs, hidden corporate bursaries, and incredible degree apprenticeships were often scattered across dozens of unorganized government directories and obscure job boards.

My Mission

My objective is to ensure that geography never limits ambition. By consolidating live opportunities into a single, lightning-fast dashboard, students across rural and industrial communities can access the exact same network infrastructure as those in major metropolitan hubs.
