Let's Connect

Hello, I'm Austin! 👋

I am a Year 12 student from rural Carmarthenshire studying Maths, Further Maths, Physics, Chemistry, and the Welsh Baccalaureate. My journey into STEM has always been driven by a deep love for problem-solving and innovation.

I originally set my sights on joining the Navy. When a setback meant that path was no longer an option, I decided to change my perspective: If I couldn't serve on their submarines, I was determined to design them. That realisation sparked my absolute passion for Nuclear Engineering.

I am a hands-on creator. Outside the classroom, I've developed an AI internet scanner to discover new engineering startups and built an AI-powered questionnaire generator to help students revise. I also regularly tinker with Raspberry Pi and Arduino builds to bring my coding concepts to life.

Alongside completing rigorous assignments for the Cambridge STEM SMART and Sutton Trust UK-US programmes, I am deeply passionate about men's mental health advocacy—having written a major essay, delivered presentations, and built websites to raise awareness around the crisis. To recharge, I swim and play rugby competitively, and enjoy leisurely games of golf and tennis.

🚀

My Ultimate Goal

To work on the cutting-edge of the energy sector—whether that is operating inside a nuclear reactor, designing next-generation fusion technology, or engineering the nuclear engines that power submarines.
